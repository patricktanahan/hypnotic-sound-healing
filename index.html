My Approach

Healing begins with feeling safe, supported, and truly heard.

Every person who walks through my door has a unique story, and every session is designed with that in mind. My goal is to create a peaceful environment where you can let go of stress, breathe deeply, and simply be present.

"Rather than trying to fix you, I provide a space where your own natural ability to restore balance can flourish."

My sessions blend therapeutic sound with guided hypnotic relaxation to help calm the nervous system and encourage a state of deep rest. Whether you're new to holistic wellness or have years of experience, you are welcome here exactly as you are.

What Is Hypnotic Sound Healing?

Two complementary practices, woven into one deeply relaxing experience

Sound Healing

The soothing vibrations of crystal singing bowls, Tibetan bowls, tuning forks, and chimes encourage relaxation and mindfulness, gently guiding the nervous system toward rest.

Hypnotherapy

A gentle guide into a naturally relaxed state of focused awareness, where positive suggestion, visualization, and calming techniques support emotional well-being and growth.

Together, these practices create an immersive experience many clients describe as peaceful, restorative, and deeply calming.
